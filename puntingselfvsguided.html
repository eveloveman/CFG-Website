Self-Punting vs Guided Tour

Guided Tour

A guided punting tour is a special and alternative way to get an overview of Cambridge and the history of this amazing city. You relax in a punt and enjoy the view while a trained guide takes you along the river Cam and provides you with lots of information and funny stories about the colleges. A guided tour is perfect if it's your first time in Cambridge and you want to do some typical touristic things.

Self-Punting

Self puntin is a very fun and exciting way to explore the river Cam and its surroundings. You may not be as quick as on a guided tour, but therefor FUN is guaranteed.
