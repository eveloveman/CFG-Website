Punting Routes

When starting from Mill Lane you can choose between 2 different routes, the college backs or the journey towards Grantchester.

Punting along the College Backs offers a beautiful view of the College Gardens. The colleges you pass are:

  • Queens
  • Kings
  • Clare
  • Trinity Hall
  • Trinity
  • St Johns
  • Magdalene
